Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken biryani is a really populer Indian rice dish tipically served in weddings or other special occassions. It is tasty and fulfilling meal that requires great effort and time .

Ingrediants

  • Basmati rice
  • chicken
  • potatoes peeled
  • peeled tomatoes
  • yogurt
  • cinnamon stick
  • ground cardomom
  • clove
  • garam masala
  • salt
  • oil
  • chilli powder
  • black pepper powder
  • onion
  • ginger
  • garlic
  • mint
  • green chilli
  • liquid curd

Steps

  1. Gather all ingrediants
  2. Heat two tablespoon of oil and fry potatoes in the oil until lightly brown
  3. Add two table spoon of oil along with ginger,garlic onion all nicely chopped. Cook and stir until onion is soft and golden.Add tomatoes,chilli powder pepper powder,salt,turmeric and coock for 5 minutes.
  4. Add yougurt,cinamon stick,ground cardomom, and hot water and cover and cook in low heat
  5. Add chicken and cover and cook for 35 to 45 minutes until little gravy left
  6. While the chicken is cooking prepare the rice. Wah and rinse it for about 5 minutes
  7. Heat oil and cook onion until golden brown.Add cinamon stick,clove,cardomom and stir in rice with hot water until all spices are mixed well. Add salt
  8. When rice half boiled add chicken mixture and bring to full boil
  9. Reduce heat to very low and cover it with a tight lid and let it steam for 20 minutes without opening or stirring
  10. During this lets prepare the side salad. Take thinly chopped onion and green chilly and mix it with curd and salt
  11. serve the tasty biryani warm with the salad,lime pickle and pappadam
